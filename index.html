What is Drallcraft

Drallcraft is a Minecraft server, where you can find friends and play together. With economy it feels more like the normal life, but you might find some weird things too. To life the life of Waffletown's citizens, claim your own plot, and start gaining resources in our great big resource world, where are you protected against enemys, such as zombies or skeletons. You can build everything you want and make your own shop to get money for special things... You can also explore the big world of Terra, a 12000x12000 block big custom created world, where you can experience the middle between minecraft and reality. We are looking forward to see you
